About Me
I'm Puneeth Y & a

Hey there! I'm a final-year Computer Science student who's borderline obsessed with making computers do stuff on their own. I spend way too much time convincing Docker, Kubernetes, and AWS to cooperate like a dysfunctional but lovable tech family. My proudest achievement? Crafting CI/CD pipelines so smooth, they might just file their own taxes.

When I'm not trying to automate everything, you’ll find me wandering around with a camera, attempting to convince wildlife to pose for me. Spoiler: they rarely listen.

Ability

My skills

I speak fluent HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and Golang — basically, my keyboard and I are on a first-name basis. I’ve dabbled in React, .NET, Django, and Node.js, usually trying to make them play nice with each other.

I’ve also wrangled Kubernetes, Helm, Terraform, Prometheus, and Grafana — you know, the usual suspects for making apps run smoothly and complain less.

On the data side, I’ve worked with MSSQL and MongoDB, and I even dipped my toes into AWS — and honestly, I kind of enjoyed the cloud fuss.

Curious for more? Feel free to check out my CV along the way!
